you'll need a webgl capable browser

Controls: WASD, Mouse Aim, Left Click to shoot at big balls.

Instructions:
* get all 3 keys
* doors can be opened with the keys (run against them)
* look for the donuts (portals) to reach other parallel worlds
* check the time - all the things you do in one world, happen in other worlds


Story:
You’re trapped and have to find all keys, to get out.
Some keys are behind locked doors (which you can open with one of other keys).

Sounds easy, but the same world exists multiple times in an multiverse scenario – and there are the other keys.
You reach them by going through portals.

But there is one more thing, you know.. the time .. Now, go, and get the right key, while it’s there – maybe in another world, you have already taken it..

disclaimer: sorry, no sound, no special effects - just game :/
